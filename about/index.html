About

About time!

The WootCat Church was created to help answer questions about God, religion, morality, etc. For example, Does god exist? Where does morality come from? What happens when we die?

We do provide answers to these questions, but our real goal is teach the idea of philosophical analysis, i.e., the love of wisdom. So ultimately you must decide on your own, you must learn to think for yourself.
