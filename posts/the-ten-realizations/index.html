The Ten Realizations

Ten Realizations

  1. Humans are easy to fool (especially you).

  2. Science is the best tool we have for understanding the world.

  3. Very few things are certain, but some things we do know.

  4. Today’s authority soon becomes yesterday’s snot-nosed kid.

  5. The world is full of beautiful people and interesting things.

  6. Eat, drink and be merry, but not too much if you have to get up and work in the morning.

  7. Before your dad’s sperm met your mom’s egg, that’s what it was like to be dead.

  8. All things are funny, in context.

  9. Looks like we’re missing one.
